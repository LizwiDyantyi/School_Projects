About the Makuphilwe Business...

Makuphilwe is a healing-focused business, based in Diepkloof, Soweto.
It was founded in 2023, by the native Gogo/Mkhulu Mashiyamahle.
She completed her training in March 2023, and has been operating in the heart of Soweto since then.
The Makuphilwe business focuses on holistic, herbal, religious, and traditional healing.
We offer a wide range of products and services, from bath salts, healing herbs, and light cleansing rituals- all at the request of the customer!
At Makuphilwe, we focus on the individual customer's needs and wants, and we cater for all of the needs of the customer. Not only do we cater for the African Traditional Religion, but for the Christian Religion as well.
With us, you are guaranteed a safe and secure healing experience.
We follow all health and safety regulatins, including COVID-19 protocols.
